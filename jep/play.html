Instructions

You will first be given a group of random categories allowed to choose a question, each question being worth one, two, or three points, points being doubled each round.

You will then move onto to the next round wherein you will attempt to answer 3 questions, after which you will move onto the final round.

The final round is one question from the category worth the most points. The player that has the highest score at the end will be displayed on home page.

If you get lost or confused, click on the link in "Instructions" heading above and come back. You should feel much better afterward.
